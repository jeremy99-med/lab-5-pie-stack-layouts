Visualizing Films from 1995 to 2018

This project visualizes box office gross revenue trends across film genres from 1995 to 2018 using three complementary chart types. The donut charts compare genre distribution for three key years (1997, 2005, 2013), showing each genre's percentage of total revenue. The stacked bar chart displays the proportional contribution of each genre to annual box office revenue over time, normalized to 100%. The streamgraph reveals how different genres' absolute revenues have grown and shifted over the entire period, with flowing curves that emphasize temporal patterns in audience preferences and market dynamics.

Data Source: MakeoverMonday 2020/W21
